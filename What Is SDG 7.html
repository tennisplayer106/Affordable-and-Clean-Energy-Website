What Is SDG 7?

SDG 7 is one of the 17 goals set by the United States which are to be accomplished by 2030. SDG 7 has five 'outcome objectives': equal access to modern energy; increasing the global share of renewable energy; doubling energy efficiency gains; encouraging access to sustainable energy science, technologies and investment; and extending and enhancing energy resources for developed countries. But the rising danger of Energy Wastage will devastate our planet if it is not stopped.
